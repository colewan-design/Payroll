Privacy Policy

The CBOO system is committed to protecting the privacy of the personal information of University employees. The following privacy policy applies to the CBOO system and outlines how we collect, use, and protect the personal information of University employees.

1. Collection of personal information:

The CBOO system collects personal information from University employees in order to prepare their payrolls. This personal information includes, but is not limited to, names, addresses, dates of birth, social security numbers, and bank account information.

2. Use of personal information:

The personal information collected by the CBOO system is used exclusively for the preparation of payrolls. This includes calculating salaries, deductions, and tax withholdings, as well as transferring funds to the appropriate bank accounts.

3. Protection of personal information:

The CBOO system takes all necessary measures to protect the personal information of University employees. This includes implementing secure protocols for storing and transmitting data, as well as regularly updating our security systems to protect against unauthorized access or misuse of personal information.

4. Sharing of personal information:

The CBOO system does not share personal information with any third parties, unless required to do so by law or with the explicit consent of the employee.

5. Access to personal information:

University employees have the right to access and review their personal information held by the CBOO system. If an employee believes that their personal information is incorrect or needs to be updated, they may contact the CBOO system to request the necessary changes.

6. Changes to this privacy policy:

The CBOO system reserves the right to update this privacy policy as needed to reflect changes in our data collection and usage practices. Any updates to this privacy policy will be posted on this page.

By using the CBOO system, University employees are agreeing to the terms of this privacy policy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact the CBOO system.
